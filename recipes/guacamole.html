Guacamole

Description

This recipe is made purely on faith. If you do not have this, the other ingredients will be of no use to you.

If you do have faith, you'll need the following ingredients:

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados
  • 1 lime
  • salt, lots of it
  • 1 diced onion of any size
  • 2 to 20 tomatoes, diced and stirred
  • As much garlic as you have in the house, minced
  • A pinch of cayenne pepper (obligatory)

Steps

  1. Prepare yourself
  2. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic. Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.
